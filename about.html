ABOUT Chazy's Food House

Chazy's Food House is Craving delicious Filipino food cooked with love? Look no further than Chazy's Food House! We're a family-run restaurant located in Tuble, Moalboal, Cebu, bringing the warmth of Cebuano cuisine straight to your plate. Our dishes are prepared using fresh, local ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations. Whether you're a local resident or just visiting the area, Chazy's Food House is the perfect place to experience the taste of Cebu. Come savor our flavorful dishes and friendly service in a welcoming atmosphere. We look forward to serving you!
